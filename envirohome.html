Climate Change
Today, climate change is one of the most severe problems in the world. According to National Geographic, 11% of global greenhouse gases are caused by deforestation. Around 800 million people are currently vulnerable to climate change. The effects of climate change can range from droughts, floods, heatwaves, and other significant types of natural disasters.
