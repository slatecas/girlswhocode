Climate
weather conditions of a particular area over a long span of time

What's the Issue?


Climate change occurs when Earth's climate system goes through adjustments resulting in new weather patterns. These patterns take place anywhere from a couple decades to millions of years. There are five major components of Earth's climate system; The atmosphere(air), hydrosphere(water), cryosphere(ice), biosphere(living things), and lithosphere(the crust and upper mantle). All five aspects of the climate system are undergoing harm through pollution, global warming, extinction, and more.
