Climate
weather conditions of a particular area over a long span of time



What's the Issue?


Climate change occurs when Earth's climate system goes through adjustments that results in new weather patterns that takes place anywhere from a couple decades to millions of years. There are 5 major components of Earth's climate system; The atmosphere(air), hydrosphere(water), cyrosphere(ice), biosphere(living things), and lithosphere(the crust and upper mantle). All 5 aspects of the climate system are undergoing harm through pollution, global warming, extinction, and more.
