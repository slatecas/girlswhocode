Plastics

Instead of using plastic bags at the grocery store, bring your own reusable bags to hold your groceries. Also, make sure to recycle objects labeled as recycable. This could really help out the environment and save our animals! Do you like to use straws? Try getting a stainless steel or glass straws. As you know, straws cannot be broken down naturally by bacteria. Instead, they break into smaller particles, which then release chemicals into the air and water. These chemicals are harsh towards animals, plants, people, and our environment.
