Plastics

Are you sure you’re recycling plastic water bottles right? Do you actually take off the bottle cap before recycling it? Do you remove the plastic seal around the water bottle? If you haven’t been doing that, then you weren’t really recycling anything. You just made it worse for the environment. Instead of using plastic bags at the grocery store, bring your own reusable bags to hold your groceries. You should also use reusable cups/bottles instead of drinking from plastic water bottles. Another thing is to recycle anything that is plastic. This could really help out the environment and save our animals! Do you like to use straws? Try getting a stainless steel or glass straws. As you know, straws cannot be broken down naturally by bacteria. Instead, they break into smaller particles, which then release chemicals into the air and water. These chemicals are harsh towards animals, plants, people, and our environment.
