What Can You do to Help?

Plastic pollution, water pollution, organic waste, and an excessive use of fossil fuels have serious consequences on society. If we continue to be careless and wasteful, there will be nothing left in the future. We need more people to notice what is happening in the world and to be aware of what they can do to prevent this. Hopefully that will start with you. There are several ways you can get involved. If you are interested in national parks, click any link below that catches your interest. In addition, you can click on one of the four boxes to the side of the screen. These buttons contain all of the information you need to start helping the environment.
