Our Mission

Here at GreenPath our mission statement is to inform those living in the United States about the dangers of excess waste in our environment, and how each individual person can tackle this issue. We plan to make a difference by ensuring everyone is knowledgeable about the ground they walk on.

Our Vision

Here at GreenPath our vision statement is to create a better, more environmentally friendly United States. We hope to live in a country where all people are properly informed about the Earth, and why it is important to take care of it. Our goal is to have all people earn genuinely high scores on our "How Eco-Friendly are You?" quiz.

Our Core Values

Ownership

We believe that every human being should show ownership for what they have contributed to the environment -both good and bad- and what they will do to better educate themselves on how to improve the surroundings.

Sustainability

We believe that in order for our company, and the US, to reach our goals we must practice sustainability - the avoidance of the reduction of our natural resources.

Optimism

We believe that the hopefulness and confidence in people's hearts will allow for change to occur in our current and future environment.
