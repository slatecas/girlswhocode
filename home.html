In 1973, President Richard Nixon sign the Endangered Species Act. There are two types of animal listed on this act: threatened and endangered. Threatened species are close to being an endangered species. An endangered species means that a certain group of animals is in danger of being extinct. Once these species are extinct, they will no longer exist. This website is dedicated to saving the innocent animals at risk.
