Organic Waste

You’re probably someone who goes to the grocery store and gets more items than what they need. Organic waste is something that is biodegradable which either comes from a plant or an animal. Organic waste is known to be broken down by other organisms over time. Buying too much food can definitely affect climate change. A way to prevent that, is to only buy what you need. You should also think about using your freezer, to store and preserve food that you won’t use for a while. In America, leftovers are usually thrown away, creating food waste. We desperately need to stop this issue. Leftovers can be used for work or school, especially if you’re late for either. Another way to prevent organic waste, is to limit your food. You should limit your diet to vegetables, fruits, rice, beans, etc. Notice that meat isn’t on the list, the reason being is because of meat waste. Meat waste is generally worse than vegetable waste. Today, ⅓ of all the food in the world is going to waste. All of the food going to waste could be enough calories to feed every undernourished person on Earth.
